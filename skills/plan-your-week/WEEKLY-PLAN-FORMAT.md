# Weekly Plan Format

```markdown
# Week of <date>

## Fixed reality
- Date/time — commitment — preparation or travel

## Outcomes
1. Outcome — why this week — finish line

## Daily bridge
### Monday
- Must happen:
- Prepare for tomorrow:
- Buffer:

## Waiting and follow-up
- Person/item — next check date

## Not this week
- Preserved item — why deferred

## Next review
- Date, time, and trigger
```

Do not fill every day. A plan without buffer is a wish list.
