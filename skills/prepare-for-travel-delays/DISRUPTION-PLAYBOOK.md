# Travel Disruption Playbook

For each relevant branch, write the first three actions and the verified channel used.

## Delay threatens connection

Check whether the booking is protected, notify the operator through its official channel, and identify the last safe onward option before rushing.

## Cancellation

Confirm status in the official app/site or at the staffed desk, preserve the notice and receipts, then compare rebooking, accommodation, and refund options under current terms.

## Missed connection

Move to the responsible operator or booking channel, state whether bags are checked through, and secure the next transport and safe overnight plan.

## Lost or delayed bag

Report before leaving the arrival area when required, preserve the reference, confirm delivery contact, and use the critical carry-on plan.

## Dead or lost phone

Use printed or second-device references, a memorised or written trusted contact, staffed help, and account-protection steps from another trusted device when needed.

## Closed onward transport

Use the pre-verified accommodation or safe waiting fallback. Do not accept unverified transport solicitations created by the disruption.
