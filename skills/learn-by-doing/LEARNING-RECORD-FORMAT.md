# Learning Record Format

Store records under `learning-records/` as `NNNN-<dash-case-title>.md`.

```markdown
# <What the learner can now do or understands differently>

<One to three sentences: the learning and why it changes the next lesson.>

## Evidence
- Attempt or explanation that demonstrated it

## Next implication
- What this unlocks or what still needs practice
```

Write a record for demonstrated understanding, relevant prior knowledge, or a corrected misconception. Exposure alone is not evidence. Do not duplicate a fact already captured in another record.
