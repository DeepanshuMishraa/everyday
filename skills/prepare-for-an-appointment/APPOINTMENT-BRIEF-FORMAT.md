# Appointment Brief Format

```markdown
# Appointment brief — <date>

## Why I am here
<one sentence>

## What I hope to leave with
- Primary outcome

## Relevant timeline
- Date — observed event, action, or record

## Bring
- [ ] Document or record — why it matters

## Ask first
1. Question
2. Question
3. Question

## If time allows
- Question

## Access or communication needs
- Need and requested accommodation

## Before I leave
- [ ] I understand the decision or explanation
- [ ] I know who owns each next step
- [ ] I have dates and written instructions
- [ ] I know when and how to follow up
```
