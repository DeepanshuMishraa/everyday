# Care Board Format

```markdown
# Care board — <person or animal> — <date range>

## Preferences and boundaries
- What matters to the care recipient:
- Consent/privacy limits:
- Professional instructions to follow exactly:

| Date/time | Need or appointment | Owner | Backup | Supplies/info | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Supplies
- Item — current amount — reorder point — owner

## Questions for professionals
- Question — who to ask — by when

## Coverage gaps
- Gap — consequence — action
```
