# Repair Brief Format

```markdown
# Repair request: <observable problem>

**Location:**
**First noticed:**
**Current state:** active / intermittent / stopped / worsening

## Observed facts
- Time — observation

## Evidence
- Photo/video/error code/model number

## Actions already taken
- Safe containment or normal control used

## Safety screen
- Hazard signals checked and result

## Access and contact
- When the area is accessible
- Person to contact

## Questions
- What should remain switched off or unused?
- What response time should I expect?
```

Do not include a confident cause unless a qualified person has confirmed it.
