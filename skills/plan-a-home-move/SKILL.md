# Plan a Home Move

Build a **command plan** around the move date. Protect access to people, documents, medication, keys, and the first night's essentials before optimising boxes.

## Make the date spine

Collect origin, destination, move date, access and handover dates, travel, vendor windows, building rules, people and animals moving, accessibility needs, budget, and current packing state.

Place fixed dates first. Flag gaps such as losing access before the moving vehicle arrives.

## Create workstreams

Track separately:

- property and handover
- transport and vendors
- packing by destination zone
- utilities and connectivity
- address and account changes
- people, pets, school, or care
- first-night setup

Assign owners and dates. Ownership includes noticing and follow-up.

## Protect essential access

Keep identity and travel documents, medication, keys, payment, chargers, basic clothing, hygiene, sleep items, and critical care supplies personally accessible. Do not place irreplaceable items in unaccompanied freight.

## Build moving day backward

Use [MOVING-PLAN-FORMAT.md](MOVING-PLAN-FORMAT.md). Name who holds keys, documents property condition, directs movers, manages dependents, travels with essentials, and receives at the destination.

Ask the country before stating address, tenancy, utility, tax, or hazardous-material rules. Treat legal disputes and contracts as matters for qualified local guidance.
