# Final Packing Check

Run immediately before departure:

- identity and travel documents match the itinerary
- prescribed medication and required documentation are personally accessible
- keys, wallet, payment backup, phone, and charging are present
- tickets, addresses, and essential contacts are available offline
- first-day clothing and hygiene survive a delayed checked bag
- liquids, batteries, tools, and restricted items match current operator and authority rules
- bag closes, meets dimension and weight limits, and retains a small buffer
- home shutdown tasks are complete
- wear-in-transit items are set aside

Do not mark an item confirmed unless the user physically verifies it.
