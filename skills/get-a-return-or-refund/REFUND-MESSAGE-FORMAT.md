# Refund Message Format

```text
Subject: Request for <remedy> — order <reference>

Hello,

I purchased <item/service> on <date> for <amount>. <One-sentence factual problem>.

Your <listing/policy/message> stated: “<short exact wording>”. I have attached <evidence>.

I am asking for <one specific remedy>. Please confirm the next step and expected completion date by <reasonable date>.

Regards,
<name>
```

On escalation, add the prior-contact dates and ask the recipient to review the existing record. Do not rewrite the story with new claims.
