# Bill Calendar Format

```markdown
# Bill calendar — <month and currency>

## Assumptions
- Confirmed income:
- Estimated items:
- Minimum buffer:

| Date | In / out | Item | Amount | Method | Running confirmed amount | Reminder |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---:|---|

## Pressure points
- Date range — reason — amount or information to confirm

## Irregular expenses
- Item — likely date — estimate status

## User decisions
- Autopay preference, provider contact, or date to confirm
```

Never display a running amount as accurate when variable essentials or pending transactions are missing.
