# Effort Guide

Use plain effort language unless the user already uses RPE or repetitions in reserve.

- **Easy:** breathing and technique remain comfortable; many repetitions or minutes remain.
- **Moderate:** clearly working, but pace and technique are stable.
- **Hard with reserve:** challenging while the user believes they could complete roughly two or three more clean repetitions, or continue briefly at the same pace.
- **Maximal:** no meaningful reserve. Do not introduce maximal work as a time-saving device.

The rating is the user's observation, not a diagnosis. Adjust down when sleep, heat, illness recovery, unfamiliar equipment, or unusual fatigue makes normal work feel harder. Stop for concerning symptoms or pain rather than assigning an effort score to them.
