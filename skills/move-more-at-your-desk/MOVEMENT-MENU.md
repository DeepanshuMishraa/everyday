# Desk-Day Movement Menu

Offer only comfortable, familiar options that fit the setting.

## Twenty to sixty seconds

- stand and sit with control
- look at a distant point, then blink normally
- change where the feet and arms rest
- gently move shoulders, hands, ankles, or neck through a comfortable range
- refill water or place an item away from the desk

## One to three minutes

- walk to another room, corridor, or outdoor point
- stand during an audio-only call
- perform several comfortable bodyweight sit-to-stands or wall-supported movements
- alternate a seated task with a standing task when the workspace allows

## Quiet or camera-on

- shift sitting position
- place both feet differently
- relax grip and jaw
- look beyond the screen while listening
- stand only if it does not distract or violate meeting expectations

Avoid forced stretches, painful ranges, breath holding, and claims that an action corrects posture or treats pain.
