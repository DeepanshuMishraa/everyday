# Decision Matrix Format

```markdown
# Purchase decision

## Requirements
- Must:
- Prefer:
- Deal-breakers:
- Budget ceiling:

| Criterion | Weight 1–5 | Candidate A | Evidence | Candidate B | Evidence |
|---|---:|---:|---|---:|---|

## Unknowns
- Unknown — how to verify — decision impact

## Premortem
- Failure — early sign — safeguard

## Rule
Choose <option> if <observable condition>; otherwise <wait/test/repair>.
```

Weights express the user's priorities. Do not tune them after seeing which candidate wins.
