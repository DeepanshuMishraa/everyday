# Total Cost of Ownership

Consider only categories relevant to the item:

- purchase price, tax, delivery, installation
- required accessories or compatibility changes
- energy, fuel, consumables, subscriptions
- routine maintenance and likely repair exposure
- insurance, storage, financing already chosen by the user
- downtime or replacement costs
- disposal and plausible resale value

Use a time horizon matching expected ownership. Show ranges for uncertain costs and identify the assumption with the greatest effect. Do not turn a rough estimate into false precision.
