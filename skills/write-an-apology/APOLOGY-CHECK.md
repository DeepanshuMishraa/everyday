# Apology Check

Remove or rewrite any line that:

- says “I am sorry if…” or “I am sorry you felt…”
- uses intention to cancel impact
- describes the apologiser's pain at greater length than the harm
- asks for immediate forgiveness, reassurance, secrecy, or a reply
- blames stress, alcohol, another person, or the recipient
- promises change without a mechanism
- offers a grand gesture that does not address the actual impact

Confirm that the message names one action, one impact, responsibility, repair, and change. Short is acceptable when all five are real.
