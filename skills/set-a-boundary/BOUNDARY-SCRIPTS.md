# Boundary Script Patterns

Adapt the pattern; do not copy a tone that does not fit the relationship.

## Availability

> I am not available for work messages after 7 p.m. I will respond the next working day.

## Repeated request

> I cannot take this on. If you ask again, my answer will still be no.

## Conversation limit

> I want to discuss this, but not while we are shouting. I am ending this conversation and can try again tomorrow at 10.

## Visit or access

> Please arrange visits with me first. If you arrive without checking, I will not open the door.

## Privacy

> I am not discussing that topic. If it continues, I will leave the conversation.

## Broken record

> I understand you disagree. My decision is the same.

Keep explanations shorter than the limit. Follow-through language describes the user's action, not punishment.
