## Design Context

### Users

People who use AI for recurring parts of everyday life and want a reliable process without rereading a book or reconstructing it from memory. They arrive with a concrete situation and want to act now.

### Brand Personality

Practical, calm, and rigorous. Everyday should reduce cognitive load without turning ordinary life into productivity theatre.

### Aesthetic Direction

An editorial, monochrome field guide with restrained category accents and a clear catalog structure. Use Zodiak for expressive editorial headings, Sentient for readable prose, and JetBrains Mono for compact interface labels, commands, and source code. Avoid generic SaaS gradients, decorative dashboards, emoji-like category markers, gamification, and technical jargon in the primary path.

### Design Principles

1. Lead with the human situation, not the package format.
2. Make browsing the single obvious action on the homepage.
3. Let people use a workflow before showing agent installation details.
4. Describe evidence honestly; never imply unmeasured quality.
5. Keep core paths keyboard-accessible, readable at 200% zoom, and usable on mobile.
